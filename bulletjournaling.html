This page is showcasing some of the bullet journal spreads that I have made over the past few years. If you are not sure what bullet journaling is, it is journaling that is done in a grid-like journal. Bullet journalist use pens, pencils, and other coloring supplies to create spreads for their daily life, memories, and much more. Journalists have a lot of creative freedom.
